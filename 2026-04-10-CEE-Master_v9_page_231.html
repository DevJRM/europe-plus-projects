Project Summary

A comprehensive 10-year modernization project to replace the unit control systems of the primary and secondary circuits on all four generating units at the Dukovany NPP to ensure operation until at least 2045-2047.

Sponsor

ČEZ Group

  • Ministry of Finance of the Czech Republic (CZ)
