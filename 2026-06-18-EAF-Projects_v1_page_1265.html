Project Summary

Development of a key international passenger node and multimodal hub in Panevėžys. The project involves the construction of a modern rail station and a dual-gauge railway junction to integrate the Rail Baltica 1435 mm high-speed line with the regional 1520 mm network, facilitating cross-border connectivity.

Financial Structure

Financing Status

not yet secured

While the mainline infrastructure is funded by CEF grants, the specific commercial tranches for the Panevėžys station superstructure represent a financing gap. No commercial debt for the station building has been finalized as of April 2026.

ECA/MLA Involvement

eligible

Eligible for ECA-backed commercial lending to cover the financing gap for station buildings, likely involving ECAs from supplier home countries (e.g., EKN for Sweden, Bpifrance for France).

Project Status

The project is in the technical design and land acquisition phase as of April 2026. Design tenders were launched in Q1 2026. Land acquisition for the Panevėžys node is currently being implemented by selected service providers.

Research Notes

Project remains in pre-construction/tender phase. Main construction contracts for the station are expected to be tendered in late 2026. Suppliers currently listed are potential candidates based on pre-qualification patterns.

Issues & Risks

Complexity of urban land acquisition; synchronization of dual-gauge technical standards; reliance on continuing EU CEF budget allocations.
