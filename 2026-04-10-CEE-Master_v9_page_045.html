Project Summary

A strategic infrastructure program to upgrade specific road sections and bridges along the North-South axis (Alexandroupolis-Burgas-Varna-Constanta) to meet NATO military mobility standards (MLC 70/100). The project ensures that heavy armored divisions and logistics convoys can transit rapidly across Bulgarian territory.

Project Scope

Bridge Reinforcement €144.0m (60.0%)
Structural strengthening of 14 key bridges to support heavy military hardware (tanks/transporters).
Bypass & Access Roads €60.0m (25.0%)
Construction of military-priority bypasses around congested urban areas.
Logistics Staging Areas €36.0m (15.0%)
Hardened parking and refueling areas for military convoys.
