Project Summary

Construction of a second concentrator plant (95,000 tpd capacity) at the Centinela mining district to increase production by 144,000 tonnes of copper per year (170,000 tonnes copper-equivalent). The project includes the development of the Encuentro Sulphides pit and a new tailings storage facility.
