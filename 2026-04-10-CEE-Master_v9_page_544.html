Project Summary

Development of a 900 MW fixed-bottom offshore wind farm in the Polish Economic Exclusive Zone (Area 14.E.1). The project is a key component of Poland's energy transition, awarded a 25-year Contract for Difference (CfD) in the December 2025 auction at 476.88 PLN/MWh. It is currently in the Front-End Engineering Design (FEED) phase following the appointment of a specialized engineering consortium in March 2026.
