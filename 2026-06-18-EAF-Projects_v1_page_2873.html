Project Summary

Major brownfield expansion of the Lumwana copper mine to transform it into a 'super pit', doubling throughput to 54Mtpa and increasing annual production to 240,000 tonnes of copper. The project extends mine life to 2057 and includes a critical power partnership with ZESCO for grid stability.
