Project Summary

Expansion of Terminal 2 capacity at Václav Havel Airport Prague, including the construction of a new Pier D with additional aircraft stands and the expansion of the terminal headhouse to increase passenger handling capacity to 21 million by 2030.

Sponsor

Letiště Praha, a. s.

Guarantor

SOE

The project is expected to be backed by the airport operator's own balance sheet and future revenues, though the Czech State (Ministry of Finance) provides implicit support as 100% shareholder.

Financial Structure

Financing Status

not yet secured

Main project financing remains unfunded. The airport operator strategy anticipates a material financing gap of several hundred million euros to be covered by commercial bank loans. No EIB loan has been formally approved as of April 2026.

ECA/MLA Involvement

eligible

Project is eligible for EIB financing under EU transport infrastructure criteria, but no direct loan is confirmed. ECA cover (e.g., Euler Hermes, SACE) would be eligible for international vendor tranches.

Project Status

Detailed design phase progressed throughout 2025; Construction Manager (Hill International JV) appointed in late 2025/early 2026. Tenders for main construction works are being prepared for launch in late 2026.

Research Notes

Issues & Risks

Coordination with the parallel airport rail link construction and management of inflation-driven cost escalations in large-scale civil works.

Updates

2026-04: Construction Manager (Hill International JV) fully onboarded; procurement strategy for main civil works being finalized.
