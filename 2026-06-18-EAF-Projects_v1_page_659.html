Project Summary

26 km commuter rail service connecting central Santiago with northern communities (Quilicura, Lampa, Batuco). The project involves a US$950M total investment, divided into an awarded surface section and an underground segment (Mapocho-Quinta Normal) currently in planning/tendering.
