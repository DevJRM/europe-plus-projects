Project Summary

A massive mixed-use 'city-within-a-city' development near Sofia based on the 15-minute city concept. The project includes a private science and tech park (15 buildings total), a residential park, and extensive educational and sports infrastructure. Following the initial BGN 135M investment for Phase 1, the sponsor committed a further BGN 265M for Phases 2-5 to reach a total project value of BGN 400M.

Financial Structure

Financing Status

Phases 1-3 funded internally by the sponsor. A financing gap of ~EUR 80M remains for the later commercial/tech park Phases 4-5, for which the sponsor is open to commercial bank lending.

Project Status

Phase 1 complete as of late 2025. Phase 2 (two tech buildings) and Phase 3 residential are currently under construction. Total completion targeted for 2028-2029.

Research Notes

Total project investment BGN 400M (approx. EUR 204M). Distinct from 'Residential Park Sofia'. Current as of April 2026.

Issues & Risks

Infrastructure connectivity to central Sofia (Sofia Ring Road capacity); competition for multinational tenants from Sofia Tech Park.

Updates

2025-12: Phase 1 received occupancy permits; Phase 2 construction confirmed progressing without delays.
