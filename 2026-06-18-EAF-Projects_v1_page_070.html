Project Summary

An integrated greenfield mine, rail, and port project in Guinea. The scope involves a 60 Mtpa iron ore mine (Blocks 3 & 4), a 70km rail spur connecting to a 536km trans-Guinean rail line, and a deep-water transhipment vessel port at Morebaya. While coordinated through a Perth-based engineering hub, the physical assets and logic are entirely Guinea-based. Operations officially commenced in November 2025, with first shipments departing in December 2025 and ramping up toward full capacity through 2026-2028.
