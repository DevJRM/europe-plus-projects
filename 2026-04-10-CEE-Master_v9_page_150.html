Project Summary

An auxiliary infrastructure modernization program at the Plovdiv North TPP (TEC Sever) site. The project involves the decommissioning of old steam units PG3 and PG4 and the installation of replacement systems including a 7-10 MVA electric steam generator for deaeration, new gas regulation stations with a total capacity of 7500 Nm3/h, and a modernized automated chemical water treatment facility with two 50 m3/h modules to support existing high-efficiency cogeneration operations.

Project Scope

Electrical Power Supply Reconstruction €19.5m (30.0%)
Installation of new 6kV and 400V distribution networks, new transformers (7-10 MVA), and 1205m of underground cabling.
Gas Supply System Overhaul €16.2m (25.0%)
Replacement of low-pressure gas regulation stations to serve new water-heating boilers with a capacity of 7500 Nm3/h.
Electric Steam Generation & Deaeration €16.2m (25.0%)
Installation of a new electric steam generator (7-10 MVA) and modernization of the deaeration system.
Water Treatment Plant €13.0m (20.0%)
New automated chemical water treatment and softening installation with two 50 m3/h modules.
